Have fun with your next favorite boardgame!
Did you ever have a game you really liked? Maybe there are similar games that are even better! BoardGameGeek is a site where hundreds of thousands of users have rated tens of thousands of games. With their API I've downloaded 13 million reviews (e.g. Amy gave Catan a 8). Doing some machine learning, the computer is able to cluster similar games together, based on the review patterns that it distills from the review data. For example, maybe cooperative games exist are rated high by people who like cooperative games. In this way, the computer figures out wat 'similar' means with only the users, games and ratings at it's disposal.

At Kaggle I've uploaded the data, including some exploratory data analysis and an in-depth analysis. And the code of the app on GitHub.

Try it out yourself!
